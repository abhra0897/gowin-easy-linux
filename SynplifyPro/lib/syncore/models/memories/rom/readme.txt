
The SYNCORE Rom compiler helps designers create rom models for their designs.
These Rom models are written out in HDL and can be synthesized as well as simulated.
A testbench is provided for the Rom models created by the SYNCORE Rom compiler. 

For detailed information on the SYNCORE Rom compiler and its various options, click 
the ROM Info button at the bottom of this window. 

If you have any questions or issues, please contact support at www.solvnet.com
