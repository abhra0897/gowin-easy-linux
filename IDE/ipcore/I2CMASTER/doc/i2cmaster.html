I2C MASTER

Information

Type:I2C MASTER
Vendor:GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary:   Gowin I2C Master is an I2C Master controller with a synchronous SRAM interface. It provides a low-speed, two-wire, serial bus interface that connects to the I2C bus via data pins (SDA) and clock pins (SCL) to complete data transmission and extend periphery components. Standard (up to 100 kHz) and fast (up to 400 kHz) I2C bus can be connected.
