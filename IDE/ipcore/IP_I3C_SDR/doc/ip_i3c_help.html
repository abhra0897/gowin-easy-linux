I3C SDR

Information

Type: I3C SDR
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary: GW I3C IP supports a high-speed, low-power, and other critical features that are currently covered by I2C and SPI. The structure of the IP provides registers that enable users to control and implement specific functions flexibly. This version supports base SDR mode.

Options

Option Description
STA HD Specify the hold time of start.
SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL.
SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL.
SEND AD SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL when sending address.
SEND AD SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL when sending address.
ACK SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL when ack.
ACK SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL when ack.
STO SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL when stop.
STO SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL when stop.
SEND DA SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL when sending data.
SEND DA SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL when sending data.
RCVE DA SCL LOW Specify the low level time of SCL when receiving data.
RCVE DA SCL HIGH Specify the high level time of SCL when receiving data.
SLAVE STATIC ADDRESS Specify the static address of slave.
