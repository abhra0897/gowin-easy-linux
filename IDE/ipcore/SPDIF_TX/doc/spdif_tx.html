SPDIF TX

Information

Type:SPDIF TX
Vendor:GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary:   SPDIF TX is a digital audio transmitter supporting the SPIDF(IEC60958) and AES3 standards for stereo PCM audio transmission. The SPDIF is a point-to-point protocol for serial transmission of digital audio through a single transmission line. The transmission medium can be either electrical or optical. It provides two channels for audio data. Supports any sample rate from 8kHz~192kHz.
