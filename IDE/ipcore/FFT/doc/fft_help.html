FFT

Information

Type: Fast Fourier Transform
Vendor: GOWIN Semiconductor
Summary:

Fast Fourier transforms are widely used for many applications in engineering, science, and mathematics.

A fast Fourier transform (FFT) algorithm computes the discrete Fourier transform (DFT) of a sequence, or its inverse. Fourier analysis converts a signal from its original domain (often time or space) to a representation in the frequency domain and vice versa. An FFT rapidly computes such transformations by factorizing the DFT matrix into a product of sparse factors. As a result, it manages to reduce the complexity of computing the DFT.
